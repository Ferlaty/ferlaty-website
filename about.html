History

All started with our YouTube channel named "Italy studio" which was created on 8th of February 2024. Our first video was uploaded on 23rd of March 2024. It was an instant success. Later we made our first long format video named F1 Edit: Pohoda-Sám Sebou | Italy studio. Big improvements in video quality came after participating in an edit competition of another editor. We finished almost last, but learnt from it, didn't give up. Right after this we rebranded our channel to "Ferlaty" for some reason. After this, we rapidly increased the quality of our edits and on 25th of April 2025 we released our edit named Sebastian Vettel – Viva La Vida | A Tribute (2008–2022) | F1 Edit. Later that year we started our GitHub and Racedepartment accounts, where we started to upload content. In September of 2025 we started developing our destkop applications. And in October of 2025 we launched this website.
Around late 2025 we published our the Multiple App Launcher project writen in Python. As of approaching v1.3.0 (28.3.2026), we open sourced it.
Back in late 2025 we started creating Assetto Corsa tracks and misc as well.
On 3rd of April, wee officialy moved from Netlify (ferlaty.netlify.app) to Cloudflare hosting (ferlaty.pages.dev).
